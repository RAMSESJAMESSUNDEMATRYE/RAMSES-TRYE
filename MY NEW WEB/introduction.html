A BRIF HISTORY OF THE FLAMES CYCLING CLUB SIERRA LEONE

The Flames Cycling Club is an amature cycling team base in Freetown, Sierra Leone that was established on the 17th November 2017.
It was founded by Tenesie Dixon which is the current captain of the team, and co-founders - Gebril J. Vandy, Fallah fayah and Amos Dixon.

MEANING OF CYCLING

Cycling is the use of a bicycle for sport, recretion, or trensportation. The sport of cycling consists of professinal and amature races which are held all over the world on roads, on tracks, mountains and so on..
